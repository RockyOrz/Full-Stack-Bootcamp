Coheire
=======

Users:
===============
Applicant, Employer

Functional requirements:
====================
1. Two kinds of login for both applicants and employers
2. Employers can post jobs with specified area
3. Employers are required to provide basic company info before posting the job.
4. Applicants can search for jobs with specified area
5. Applicants are required to provide personal info before applying jobs for accelerating the hiring process
6. Applicants can apply for jobs with default personal information or upload extra files.
